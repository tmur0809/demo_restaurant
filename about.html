Our story

Built on hospitality, family recipes, and local roots.

The Corner Table started as a small neighborhood gathering place where friends, families, and regulars could enjoy a warm meal without any fuss. We still believe in keeping things simple: good food, honest service, and a room that feels lived in.

We source seasonal ingredients when we can, keep our menu approachable, and make sure every plate feels like something worth sharing.

What to expect

  • Friendly service from a local team
  • Comfort food favorites and daily specials
  • Cozy dining rooms and easygoing evenings
